About Us

We're the Grind. We're here to host community inspections of new developments that are proposed in your local area. Using AR - Augmented Reality; we're going to show you what the development will look like when completed surrounded by its environment. Interested ?

How it works:

  1. Go to one of the locations where we're hosting an AR development inspection
  2. Talk to one of our hosts and grab a Microsoft Hololense - this is what lets you see AR !
  3. Inspect the development site. You'll see something that wasn't there before.
  4. Return the Hololense back to the host, who will then give you a code of which you'll use on our website - yes, the one you're on right now.
  5. Find the community project that you inspected and click on it to open its page
  6. Click on the vote button and then enter the code our host gave you
  7. Vote if you want this project to proceed or be termintated
  8. Tell us why you voted the project to proceed or be terminated
  9. Submit your vote and you're done !
